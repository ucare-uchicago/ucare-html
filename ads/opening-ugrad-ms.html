Undergraduate and Master Research in
Cloud Computing and Operating/Storage Systems

Our research group is currently recruiting undergraduate and master students who would like to get strong experience in systems research in the area of Cloud Computing and Operating/Storage Systems. In this project, students will learn about exciting technology running behind large Internet services at Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Twitter, Amazon, Yahoo!, and Amazon.

Students who participate in our projects and perform well will become co-authors in our publications. For more about our research can be found in our UCARE group website.

Requirements and contact information can be found below.

Basic Requirements

For undergraduates:
  • 1-2 year commitment
  • Interested in analyzing and building large, real-world systems
  • Already took CMSC 15400, Introduction to Computer Systems
  • Already took or currently taking CMSC 23000, Operating Systems
  • Already took or currently taking one or more of these courses:
    • CMSC 22200, Computer Architecture
    • CMSC 23300, Networks and Distributed Systems
    • CMSC 23500, Introduction to Databases
For master students:
  • Commit for a minimum of 9 months, including summer internship with us (strict requirement). Note that the MPCS program allows academic internship to be used to fulfill your internship requirement.
  • (If after graduation, you plan to do more research after your MS program, perhaps for shaping your CV for PhD admission or better job opportunities, my group is willing to host you as well (contingent on your performance).
For any students:
  • Have built some big programs before (like big class projects).
  • Fast in writing code (at least in one language).
  • Independent (willing to self-learn), consistent (long commitment), and persistent (willing to solve hard problems).
